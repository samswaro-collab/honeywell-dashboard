1. HONA spinoff creates new vendor openings: HAT as a standalone entity resets its approved vendor list. Once-in-a-decade entry opportunity for ER&D partners not previously in the HON corporate program.

3. Record $37.5B backlog drives production engineering demand: Orders grew 23% organically in Q4 2025. Production ramp across E&PS and CS requires engineering documentation, process engineering, and supplier quality support.

4. eVTOL + FLRAA first-mover position: Honeywell CS won two landmark eVTOL/military FbW contracts. Cert documentation for novel FbW architectures is a greenfield — no established vendor ecosystem. First-mover cert partner locks in multi-year relationship.
